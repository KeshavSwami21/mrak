About Campus

Our Campus:

The Institution is situated over a sprawling area of 5.1 acres. The Institute has gorgeous building. The Institution has commendable infrastructure and aesthetically designed building with beautiful landscape. The Institute has spacious and well ventilated class rooms, laboratories equipped with latest and modern machinery and equipment to impart up – to – date practical knowledge of the students.

Hostel Facility:

The Campus will offer good hostel facility for our students as its construction is under process.

Class Rooms & Drawing Hall
About Classrooms & Drawing Halls:

The classrooms and drawing hall of the institute are fully furnished with benches and white boards to ensure a steady flow of studies in different trades for the Industry.

Library
Our Library:

This institute Library is a modern library meant to fulfill the information needs of students, faculty and staff of various Departments of the Institute. One reading hall with a seating capacity of 40 has been provided with the Periodical Section where all the journals, newspapers and magazines are displayed. Library has a rich collection of various volumes in the stream of Engineering & Technology, Applied sciences etc. Library is also having magazines and newspapers for its readers.

Library Membership:

The following shall be entitled to enroll themselves as members of the library:

  1. The faculty members of various departments.
  2. Non-teaching staff of the Institute.
  3. All the students enrolled in the various departments of the Institute.
Those who are willing to become member of the Library are required to fill up library membership card duly recommended by the chairperson/ head of the department. Each member has been issued a non-transferable library card.

Library and Reading Room:

The students are allowed to use these books freely under the following regulations:

  1. Books must be returned or got renewed within 15 days, but the Librarian may summon the return to any book earlier and when urgently required.
  2. Fine as per Library rules will be imposed for every book returned late.
  3. Students will be required to pay the cost of books, if lost by them along with fine as per library rules shall also be imposed.
  4. Marking on books, defacing or tearing of paper is not permitted and the defaulters will ordinarily be required to pay the price of the book.
  5. Technical journals are provided only in the Reading Room for the students. The automation of Digital Library is under process.

Conference Hall
Our Conference Hall:

Institute has well furnished (projector, internet connectivity) Conference Hall in the Teaching Block at ground floor for Institutional meetings, Seminar, Expert Lecture and Campus Interview.The conference room is a traditional, wood panelled room, offers an intimate venue for multifold events underlaying staff meetings, seminars, short term training courses, guest lectures and other accessible events promoting students academic growth. It can also be set up in a boardroom style seating upto 60 members in tiered seats.The room is fully air conditioned and has a pleasant view of an extending greenery girded the college.

This institute established in 2019-20, as Govt. Polytechnic, PanchkulaThe Institute is spread over an area of about 5.1 acres. The campus is laid out with teaching blocks, Canteen, Play Ground, Separate Common room for Girls and Boys.
